Liu Bei


Abilities

Kindness 仁德
Liu Bei's can give any number of his hand cards to any players. If he gives away more than one card, he recovers one unit of health.
Fierce General 激将
King Ability: If Liu Bei needs to use an attack, he can ask any member of Shu to provide him one.

Clarifications

  • Remember that Liu Bei can only give away hand cards via "Kindness". He cannot give away equipped cards.

  • When Liu Bei uses "Kindness", he gains one unit of health the moment he gives the second hand card to another player. Consider the following scenario - Liu Bei gives two cards away when he has max health (four units). He then loses a Duel, reducing he current health to three. If he gives a third card card away after losing the Duel, he does not regain any health.

  • Liu Bei can use "Fierce General" when he wants to attack another player, when participating in a Duel, and when responding to Barbarians.

Guan Yu


Abilities

Fighting Saint 武圣
Guan Yu can use any red card as an attack.

Clarifications

  • Guan Yu's "Fighting Saint" allows him to use any of his red cards as Attack, whether they are equipped or in his hand. If he uses an equipped card to attack, he sacrifices the properties of the equipment.

    For example, if he has a red Crossbow and uses several red cards to attack, he cannot then discard the Crossbow to attack again, as he would be giving up the Crossbow and hence his ability to continue to attack. Similarly, he could not discard a -1 Horse to attack someone outside of his reach after the horse is discarded.

Zhang Fei


Abilities

Roar 咆哮
Zhang Fei has no restrictions on how many times he can attack during his turn.

Clarifications

  • Zhang Fei can use a weapon ability each and every time he attacks.

Zhuge Liang


Abilities

Astrology 观星
Before his judgment phase, Zhuge Liang can look at the top X cards of the deck, X either the number of characters in play or 5, whichever number is smaller. He can then rearrange the order of these cards and put as many on the bottom of the deck as he chooses.
Empty City 空城
When Zhuge Liang has no hand cards, he cannot be the target of an attack or duel.

Clarifications

  • "Empty City" will not protect Zhuge Liang if he uses his last hand card in a Duel and loses, as he had at least one hand card when he was targeted with the Duel. Also, if he is targeted with a Duel and Negates it with his last card only to have another character Negate his Negate, Zhuge Liang will still be damaged as the Duel targeted him when he had a hand card

  • "Empty City" will not protect Zhuge Liang if he uses his last Escape against the an Attack from the Axe and the attacking character uses two card to cut through the Escape. Similarly, this ability will not protect Zhuge Liang in a similar situation against the Green Dragon Blade.

  • If Diao Chan uses "Sow Discord" on Zhuge Liang when he has no hand cards, she must force the other male character to use the first Attack.

Zhao Yun


Abilities

Dragon Courage 龙胆
Zhao Yun can use attacks and escapes interchangeably.

Clarifications

  • Zhao Yun cannot use the Eight Trigrams to try to produce an Attack.

    Zhao Yun cannot use the Serpent Spear to discard two cards to produce an Escape.

Ma Chao


Abilities

Horsemanship 马术
Ma Chao always has a -1 bonus.
Iron Calvary 铁骑
When Ma Chao attacks a player, he can flip a judgment card. If it is red, the attack cannot be escaped.

Clarifications

  • Ma Chao's "Horsemanship" gives him a bonus that stacks with another -1 horse when he has one equipped (i.e. if he has a -1 horse equipped, Ma Chao has a physical range bonus of 2 and could reach someone three spaces to his right or left).

    Ma Chao's "Iron Cavalry" is activated before another character plays an Escape. This means that Ma Chao cannot wait to see if the opponent has an escape then activate the power to waste the Escape.

    If Ma Chao targets multiple opponents with the same attack, he flips a judgment card for each player individually.
